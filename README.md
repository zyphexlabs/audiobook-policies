# AudioBook — Account Deletion

**App:** AudioBook
**Developer:** zyphexlabs
**Last updated:** May 3, 2026

## How to delete your account

You can delete your AudioBook account directly from within the app:

1. Open the **AudioBook** app and sign in.
2. Navigate to your **Profile** screen.
3. Tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.

Account deletion takes effect immediately. You will be signed out of the app and your account data will be removed from our servers.

## What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following is permanently removed:

- Your profile information
- Your uploaded books and PDF files
- Your audiobook library, reading progress, and chapter state
- Your vocabulary, bookmarks, notes, and highlights
- Your subscription state and entitlement records
- Your usage history (TTS audio minutes, AI query history)
- Your local synced data on the device

## What data is retained

The following data may be retained after account deletion for legal, security, or accounting purposes:

- **Transaction records** — Google Play subscription billing receipts are retained for up to 7 years for tax and accounting compliance, as required by law.
- **Aggregate analytics** — anonymized usage statistics that cannot be tied back to your account.

## Subscription cancellation

Account deletion does **not** automatically cancel active Google Play subscriptions. To cancel your subscription:

1. Open the **Google Play Store** app on your device.
2. Tap your profile icon → **Payments and subscriptions** → **Subscriptions**.
3. Find AudioBook, tap it, and select **Cancel subscription**.

Subscription billing stops at the end of the current billing period.

## Alternative: deletion request by email

If you cannot access the in-app deletion flow (for example, you have lost access to your account credentials), you may request deletion by emailing:

**zyphexlabs@gmail.com**

Include the email address registered to your AudioBook account in your message. We will process email-based deletion requests within 30 days.
